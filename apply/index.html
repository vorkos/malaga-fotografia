Let's create something together

I collaborate with models on fine-art portrait, boudoir and nude work across Málaga and the Costa del Sol. If the images in the Journal speak to you, read the terms below and apply — I answer every serious message personally.

What TFP means here

  • No money either way. We collaborate — you give your time, I give mine.
  • You get the edited images. A gallery of finished, retouched photos to use for your own portfolio and social media.
  • Shared usage rights. You may use the images for your portfolio and personal social media; I may use them for my portfolio, this site and the Journal. Anything commercial is agreed separately, in writing.
  • You approve what's public. Nothing of you goes on the site or Journal without your say-so, and you can ask for something to be taken down later.

Safety & respect

  • 18+ only, with ID confirmed before a nude shoot — no exceptions.
  • Bring a friend or chaperone if it helps you feel comfortable. Always welcome.
  • "No" means no, at any point. We agree the plan beforehand and never push past it.
  • Your data is handled carefully. I keep only what's needed to plan the shoot and deliver your images, and I never share your contact details or real name without your consent.

No payment is involved either way. Your details are used only to arrange a possible shoot; ask any time to have them deleted.
