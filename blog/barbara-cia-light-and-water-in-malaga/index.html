Barbara Cia — Light and Water in Málaga

A sunlit morning shoot in a Málaga apartment, chasing window light, rooftop water, and unexpected corners.

We worked as a team of three, borrowing a friend’s apartment in Málaga to chase the kind of soft morning light that flatters a body honestly. Barbara, very experienced in front of the camera, carried much of the session herself — finding poses, adjusting the light, quietly mentoring my friend, who was new to both photography and modeling. We caught the rooftop pool empty and golden before the building woke up, then kept wandering, turning ordinary corners of the apartment, even the shower, into something worth photographing. It felt collaborative in the truest sense: less me directing, more the three of us discovering the light together. A simple morning that became one of my favorite shoots this year.

A calm morning, soft light, and no rush — that's when I feel most at home in front of a camera.

— Barbara Cia
